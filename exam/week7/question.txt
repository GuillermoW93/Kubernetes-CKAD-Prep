If you want to use 'jsonPath' for multiple pods (for example) you use: 
-  k get pods -n session283884 -o jsonpath={.items[*].spec.nodeName}

Look at the beginning it says: ".items[*]" that means for all items, check the .spec.nodeName

if you look at it from a single pod, you can use jsonpath={.spec.nodeName}
